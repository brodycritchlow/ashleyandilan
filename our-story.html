Our Story

It all started with a house party. What began as staying up all night watching Victorius quickly turned into something neither of us wanted to end. Over time, we’ve collected countless memories: inside jokes that make no sense to anyone else, adventures, and quiet moments that reminded us we’d found something rare. Through the fun, the laughter, and the occasional debate over what to eat for dinner, we discovered what makes us work: we balance each other, challenge each other, and bring out the best in one another. Somewhere between the laughter and the late-night talks, we realized this was it—this was the kind of love people talk about in stories, but one that’s very much ours. Now, we’re excited to celebrate the next chapter with the people who have supported, cheered, and loved us along the way.

So here we are, about to say “I do”—ready to carry our story forward with joy, adventure, and maybe just a little bit of chaos (the good kind).
